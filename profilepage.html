Bio

Ms. Deepthi S is an Assistant Professor in the Department of ISE. Her academic interests include Data Mining, Discrete and Combinatorial Mathematics, Graph Theory, Image Processing, Big Data, Database Management Systems, Unix System Programming,Software Engineering, Information Systems. In addition to her interest in academics, she also focuses on research in the area of Image Processing. She holds a B.E. degree in ISE from Visvesvaraya Technological University and an M.E degree in CSE from the Bangalore University,and currently pursuing Ph.D under Visvesvaraya Technological University. She has participated in various workshops and faculty development programs held by various colleges. She has around 16 years of teaching experience and is associated with Cambridge Institute of Technology from 2012. Guided B.E. and M.E students in their seminars and project dissertations.
