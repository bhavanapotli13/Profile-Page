Bio

Ms.Preethi S is an Associate Professor in the Department of ISE. Her academic interests include Computer Networks, Cryptography and Network Security, Analog and Digital Electronics, Microprocessors, Data Communication, Microcontroller and Embedded Systems, Computer Organization, System Modelling and Simulation, C programming. In addition to her interest in academics, she also focuses on research in the area of Image Processing. She holds a B.E. degree in CSE from Bangalore University and an M.Tech degree in CNE from the Visvesvaraya Technological University and currently pursuing Ph.D under Visvesvaraya Technological University. She has participated in various workshops and faculty development programs held by various colleges. She has around 18 years of teaching experience and is associated with Cambridge Institute of Technology from 2009. Guided B.E. and M.Tech students in their seminars and project dissertations. She has taken the responsibilities in the department as NBA Coordinator, Counselling Coordinator and involved in the technical training to students. She is associated with Oxfam India which is a non profit organization working to support child education and empowering women from 6 to 7 years and supported financially for girl child education for 7 students.
