Bio

Ms. Priyanka R is an Assistant Professor in the Department of ISE. Her academic interests include Operating Systems, Software Engineering, Storage Area Networks, Design and Analysis of algorithms, Object Oriented Modelling and design, System Software, Object Oriented Programming and Computer Networks. In addition to her interest in academics, she also focuses on research in the area of Wireless Sensor Networks. She holds a B.E. degree in CSE from Visvesvaraya Technological University and a M.Tech degree in CSE from the Visvesvaraya Technological University, and currently pursuing Ph.D under Visvesvaraya Technological University. She has participated in various workshops and Faculty Development Programs held by various colleges. She has around 6+ years of teaching experience and is associated with Cambridge Institute of Technology from 2019.Guided many projects for lower semesters and higher semester students.
